About Me

Hello! I’m Shanmukha Vardhan, a dedicated Full-Stack Developer with a strong passion for building scalable, efficient, and user-centric web applications.

My journey into tech has been unconventional yet enriching. After completing a Diploma in Electrical and Electronics Engineering, I explored the industry before discovering my true passion for software development. Since then, I have committed myself to mastering the craft.

I have completed a Full-Stack Java Development course at Cranes Varsity, earned an AngularJS certification through Infosys Springboard, and strengthened my foundation with IBM certifications in Agile Methodology and Web Development. Currently, I am pursuing a B.Tech in Computer Science and Business Systems (CSBS) at GITAM University, where I actively integrate problem-solving, technical expertise, and design thinking principles.

Beyond academics, I have applied my skills in real-world projects and completed a Full-Stack Development internship in Bangalore. My portfolio reflects my ability to create clean, modern, and effective web solutions using technologies like Node.js, AngularJS, HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.

I am continuously seeking opportunities to contribute to dynamic teams through internships and collaborations. I am also focused on refining my public speaking and leadership skills to complement my technical capabilities.

Outside of coding, I enjoy exploring emerging technologies, enhancing my design thinking skills, and maintaining a confident and positive approach to both work and life.
